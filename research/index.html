Undergraduate Research Assistant with the Office of Naval Research and the University of South Carolina

These are some of the research projects and apps I’ve built based on real-world challenges in computer vision, labeling tools, and prompt engineering. Click each card to learn more or view the source.

Raindrop Image Segmentation

Uses OpenCV and YOLOv7 to improve detection in rainy/foggy scenes by masking water droplets on lenses.

Negative Prompt Generator

Helps refine AI image generation by generating negative prompts to exclude unwanted features from results.

Image Labeling Tool (SAM2)

A GUI that generates masks using SAM2 and allows fast annotation with point-based input and preview options.
