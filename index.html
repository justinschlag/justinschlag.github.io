Problem Statement

The Strom Thurmond Fitness Center at USC experiences significant overcrowding during various hours of the day, creating an inconsistent experience for students and discouraging some from using the facility during these busy times.

Affinity Diagram

The Strom Thurmond Fitness Center at USC faces significant overcrowding during peak hours, resulting in inconsistent experiences for students. To address this challenge, I developed an affinity diagram to organize potential solutions and outline necessary steps prior to launching the new app.

Sketches

I created sketches to showcase the app's core functionality and design, focusing on intuitive user interactions and smooth screen transitions to present a visually appealing, marketable interface.

Storyboard

Paper Prototype

My paper prototype is a visual representation of my app, aimed at solving overcrowding at the Strom Thurmond Fitness Center. It features three main screens: the Crowdmeter home screen, which shows current gym occupancy, a graph screen displaying gym capacity trends; and a user profile screen for tracking fitness goals and adjusting settings.
