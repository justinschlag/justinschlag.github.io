About Me

Hi, I’m Justin Schlag, a Computer Engineering student at the University of South Carolina Honors College.

I created this website to showcase my professional life, highlight the projects I’ve worked on, and document my journey through college, internships, jobs, and research. It’s part resume, part personal timeline, and something I genuinely enjoy building. My goal is for it to help me stand out when applying for jobs and also serve as a place I can look back on with pride.

Most of my time is spent taking on new challenges, whether through job opportunities, research, or personal projects. This site helps me keep everything organized and in one place.

A little about me personally: I’m from South Carolina and enjoy fishing, working out, and spending time outside. I’m a collegiate powerlifter on USC’s Powerlifting team and stay active in the Gamecock Barbell Club. In my free time, I like working with my hands and building things — whether it’s woodworking or electronics.

If you have any questions, feel free to chat with JustinBot. If he doesn’t have the answer, my contact info is available at the bottom of the main page. I am always looking for new opportunities, so feel free to reach out!

Thanks for stopping by and learning a little bit about me.
